Benefits and Tips

Some benefits and tips of physical activity

1-What are the health benefits of exercise?
  1. Reduce the risk of heart disease
  2. Improve your mental health and mood
  3. Strengthen your bones and muscles
2-How can I make exercise part of my regular routine?
  1. Find activities you can do even in bad weather
  2. Keep track of your progress
  3. Have fun exercising
